About

Sol-Arch is a construction-led firm. We lead with shell construction, the foundations, concrete, masonry and structural frame that carry a building, and self-perform the scope that drives the schedule.

Where a project's structure and schedule are won or lost, we self-perform. Fewer handoffs, tighter control of the sequence, and one team accountable for the shell across the United States and internationally.
