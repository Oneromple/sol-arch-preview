Sol-Arch About

A one-stop design & build studio.

Started in 2003, Sol-ARCH was created with a need in Miami’s Real Estate and Design markets for photorealistic renderings, animations and scale models. In 2005 a new trend towards sustainable design was up and coming and Sol-ARCH created a division focused on designing buildings with a sensibility to the environment and in 2008 Sol-ARCH was responsible for the design and construction administration of the first retail U.S.G.B.C. LEED “GOLD” certified building in Miami-Dade County. Today Sol-ARCH is involved in Domestic and International projects that include corporate offices, retail/commercial developments, hospitality resorts, cruise terminal structures, residential-multiuse developments, educational facilities, automotive and specialty facilities. Although our focus is primarily Architecture, Sol-ARCH takes pride in its ability to bring to its clients a one stop shop for their projects with services that include initial site due diligence, financial feasibility, marketing, development, architecture and construction services.
