Sol-Arch About

A construction-led firm, built on the shell.

Sol-Arch is a construction-led firm based in Miami. We lead with construction, and specifically with shell construction: the foundations, cast-in-place concrete, masonry and structural frame that carry a building. On the scope that drives the schedule we self-perform, so the people who plan the shell are the same people in the field placing it, and there is a single point of accountability for the structure. Founded in 2003, Sol-Arch began in visualization with photorealistic renderings, animation and scale models, and in 2008 delivered the first retail USGBC LEED Gold certified building in Miami-Dade County. Today the firm works across the United States and internationally, on corporate offices, retail and commercial developments, hospitality resorts, cruise-terminal structures, multi-use residential, educational and specialty facilities, backed by in-house visualization and architecture.
